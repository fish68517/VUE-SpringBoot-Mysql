## Getting Started

### Registration

1. Click the **Register** button on the login page
2. Enter your desired username (must be unique)
3. Create a strong password
4. Select your role: **User** (for regular users)
5. Click **Register**
6. You'll be redirected to the login page
7. Log in with your new credentials

### First Login

After your first login, you'll see the home dashboard with:
- Welcome banner with your name
- Featured fitness resources
- Recent community posts
- Quick access cards for main features

## Account Management

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile page displays:
   - Username
   - Avatar
   - Gender
   - Personal introduction
   - Check-in statistics

### Updating Your Profile

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click the **Edit** button
3. Update your information:
   - **Avatar**: Click to upload a new profile picture (JPG, PNG, GIF - max 5MB)
   - **Gender**: Select from dropdown
   - **Introduction**: Write a brief bio about yourself
4. Click **Save** to apply changes
5. You'll see a success message confirming the update

### Changing Your Avatar

1. In edit mode on your profile
2. Click the avatar upload area
3. Select an image file from your computer
4. The preview will show before saving
5. Click **Save** to confirm

## Browsing Fitness Resources

### Accessing Resources

1. Click **Resources** in the sidebar menu
2. You'll see a grid of available fitness resources

### Resource Types

The system offers three types of resources:
- **Videos**: Fitness tutorials and demonstrations
- **Articles**: Written guides and tips
- **Documents**: PDFs and downloadable materials

### Filtering Resources

1. Use the **Content Type** filter dropdown
2. Select: All, Video, Article, or Document
3. Results update automatically

### Viewing Resource Details

1. Click on any resource card
2. The detail page shows:
   - Full resource content
   - Video player (for videos)
   - Document viewer or download link (for documents)
   - Article text (for articles)
   - Creator information (if created by a coach)
   - View count
   - Collection button

### Collecting Resources

1. Open a resource detail page
2. Click the **Add to Collection** button
3. The resource is saved to your collection
4. Click **Remove from Collection** to remove it later

### Viewing Your Collection

1. Click **My Collections** in the sidebar
2. See all resources you've saved
3. Click any resource to view details
4. Click **Remove** to delete from collection

## Training Plans

### Viewing Your Training Plans

1. Click **Training Plans** in the sidebar
2. See all training plans assigned to you by coaches
3. Each plan shows:
   - Plan name and description
   - Coach name
   - Start and end dates
   - Current status (Active, Completed, Cancelled)

### Understanding Training Plans

A training plan includes:
- **Exercises**: Specific workouts with sets, reps, and duration
- **Duration**: Start and end dates
- **Status**: Current state of the plan

### Viewing Plan Details

1. Click on a training plan card
2. See the full plan information
3. View the list of exercises with details:
   - Exercise name
   - Number of sets
   - Repetitions
   - Duration or weight

### Finding a Coach

If you don't have any training plans:
1. Look for the **Find a Coach** section
2. Browse available coaches
3. Click on a coach profile to see their specialties
4. Contact them to request a training plan

## Community Features

### Viewing the Community Feed

1. Click **Community** in the sidebar
2. See posts from all users in reverse chronological order
3. Each post shows:
   - User avatar and name
   - Post content
   - Images (if included)
   - Like count
   - Comment count
   - Timestamp

### Creating a Post

1. Click the **Create Post** button at the top of the feed
2. Enter your post content
3. Optionally add images:
   - Click the image upload area
   - Select up to 5 images (JPG, PNG, GIF - max 5MB each)
   - Preview shows before posting
4. Click **Post** to publish
5. Your post appears at the top of the feed

### Liking Posts

1. Find a post you like
2. Click the **Star icon** to like it
3. The like count increases
4. Click again to unlike

### Commenting on Posts

1. Click the **comment icon** on a post
2. Enter your comment text
3. Click **Post Comment**
4. Your comment appears with your avatar and name
5. The comment count increases

### Managing Your Posts

**Editing a Post:**
1. Click the **edit icon** on your own post
2. Modify the content or images
3. Click **Save**

**Deleting a Post:**
1. Click the **delete icon** on your own post
2. Confirm the deletion
3. The post is removed from the feed

**Deleting Comments:**
1. Click the **delete icon** on your own comment
2. Confirm the deletion
3. The comment is removed

## Tracking Your Progress

### Daily Check-In

Check-ins help you build consistency and track your fitness habits.

**Performing a Check-In:**
1. Click **Check-In** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Check In Today** button
3. A confirmation message appears
4. You can only check in once per day

**Viewing Check-In History:**
1. Go to the Check-In page
2. See your complete check-in history with dates
3. View your statistics:
   - **Total Check-Ins**: Total number of check-ins
   - **Current Streak**: Consecutive days checked in
   - **Longest Streak**: Your best streak ever

### Diet Tracking

Track your meals and nutrition to complement your fitness routine.

**Logging a Meal:**
1. Click **Diet Records** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Meal**
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Meal Type**: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack
   - **Food Items**: List what you ate
   - **Calories**: Total calories consumed
   - **Date**: When you ate this meal
4. Click **Save**

**Viewing Diet History:**
1. Go to Diet Records page
2. See all your logged meals organized by date
3. Click on a date to see daily summary:
   - Total calories for the day
   - Breakdown by meal type

**Editing a Meal:**
1. Click the **edit icon** on a meal entry
2. Update the information
3. Click **Save**

**Deleting a Meal:**
1. Click the **delete icon** on a meal entry
2. Confirm the deletion

**Daily Summary:**
1. On the Diet Records page, select a date
2. See total calories and meal breakdown
3. Helps you track daily nutrition goals

## Finding Content

### Using Search

The search feature helps you find resources, posts, and coaches quickly.

**Performing a Search:**
1. Click the search bar in the header
2. Enter your search query (e.g., "yoga", "cardio", "stretching")
3. Optionally select a content type filter:
   - All: Search everything
   - Resources: Only fitness resources
   - Posts: Only community posts
   - Coaches: Only coach profiles
4. Click **Search** or press Enter
5. Results appear organized by type

**Understanding Search Results:**
- Results are ranked by relevance
- Exact matches appear first
- Partial matches follow
- Each result shows type, title, and description

## FAQ

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Currently, password reset is not available. Please contact an administrator for assistance.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: No, usernames are permanent. Choose carefully during registration.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact an administrator to request account deletion.

### Resources & Training

**Q: Can I download resources?**
A: Yes, documents can be downloaded. Videos and articles are viewed online.

**Q: How do I become a coach?**
A: Contact an administrator to request a role change to coach.

**Q: Can I create my own training plan?**
A: No, only coaches can create training plans. You can request one from a coach.

### Community & Tracking

**Q: Can I edit my posts after posting?**
A: Yes, click the edit icon on your post to modify it.

**Q: How is my streak calculated?**
A: Your streak counts consecutive days with check-ins. Missing one day resets it to 0.

**Q: Can I see other users' diet records?**
A: No, diet records are private. Only you and coaches (if assigned) can see them.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Contact an administrator with details about the content.

### Technical Issues

**Q: I'm getting an error when uploading images.**
A: Ensure your image is under 5MB and in JPG, PNG, or GIF format.

**Q: The app is loading slowly.**
A: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

**Q: I can't log in. What should I do?**
A: Verify your username and password are correct. If you still can't log in, contact an administrator.
